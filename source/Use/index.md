## 🌀 Personal Logic (aka Tool Philosophy)

I like tools that:
- Don’t get in the way  
- Work across domains (research ↔ music ↔ life)  
- Are high-quality but not high-maintenance  
- Have just enough friction to feel real, but not enough to make me quit

I try not to over-optimize. But sometimes I go on YouTube and watch “10 Obsidian plugins you *must* use” and forget all that.
